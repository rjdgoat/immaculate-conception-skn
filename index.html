About Our School

Immaculate Conception Catholic School is a welcoming educational community in St. Kitts. We combine academic excellence with Catholic values to support students from early years through middle school. Our school encourages respect, service, and leadership as part of everyday learning.

Academic Programs

Our curriculum is designed to challenge students while nurturing their spiritual, social, and intellectual growth. We offer literacy, math, science, religious education, arts, and physical education with a focus on creativity, critical thinking, and compassion.

Admissions

Families are invited to learn about enrollment, school tours, and parish support. We welcome students of all backgrounds who seek a strong academic foundation grounded in Catholic faith and community values.

More About Our School

Immaculate Conception Catholic School is committed to helping every student succeed academically and grow spiritually. Our school community is built on faith, respect, and service, with strong support from families and parish leaders.
